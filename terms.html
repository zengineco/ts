Description of service

TipStreams is a client-side web application that generates QR codes encoding cryptocurrency payment request URIs. The Service accepts user-provided wallet addresses and produces scannable QR codes in standard formats such as bitcoin:address?amount=0.001, ethereum:address, dogecoin:address?amount=10, and similar schemes for supported blockchains.

The Service is provided free of charge. All computation — including QR generation, image processing, and color operations — runs entirely within your browser. No user data is transmitted to any TipStreams server in connection with QR generation. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Supported chains at launch include Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Dogecoin (DOGE), Litecoin (LTC), Solana (SOL), Monero (XMR), TRON (TRX), and BNB Chain (BNB). Supported chains may change at any time without notice.
