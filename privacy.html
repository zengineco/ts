Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how TipStreams ("we," "our," or "the Service"), operated by Zengine™, collects, uses, and shares information when you use tipstreams.com or any subdomain or path where TipStreams is hosted.

By using TipStreams, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What data we collect

Data we do not collect

TipStreams is a client-side, static web application. The following data never leaves your browser and is never transmitted to any TipStreams server:

  • Your cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Any images you upload as center overlays
  • Tip amounts or labels you enter
  • Generated QR codes
  • Any personally identifiable information you type into the generator

There is no TipStreams server, database, or backend. All QR generation, image processing, and color computation runs entirely within your web browser. When you close the tab, all session data is gone.

Data automatically collected

Like any website, TipStreams may collect standard server log data through its hosting provider (GitHub Pages / Netlify). This may include:

  • IP address (anonymized by hosting provider)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of visit
  • Referring URL
  • Operating system

This data is collected and retained by the hosting provider in accordance with their privacy policies. TipStreams does not have direct access to individual-level server log records.

Google Analytics

TipStreams does not currently use Google Analytics. If analytics are added in a future version, this policy will be updated and explicit notice will be provided.
